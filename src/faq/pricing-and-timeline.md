question_he: "כמה עולה אתר מקצועי?"
answer: |
  Website costs vary based on complexity and requirements. Here's a typical breakdown:
  
  **Basic Business Website (5-8 pages)**: ₪3,000 - ₪6,000
  - Professional design and responsive layout
  - Contact forms and basic SEO optimization
  - Content management system training
  - 2-3 weeks delivery time
  
  **E-commerce Website**: ₪6,000 - ₪12,000
  - Online store with payment integration
  - Product catalog and inventory management
  - Customer accounts and order tracking
  - 4-6 weeks delivery time
  
  **Custom Web Application**: ₪10,000+
  - Complex functionality and integrations
  - Database design and API development
  - Advanced user management systems
  - Timeline varies by project scope
  
  All projects include:
  - Free consultation and project planning
  - Mobile-responsive design
  - Basic SEO optimization
  - 30 days of post-launch support
  - Training on content management
