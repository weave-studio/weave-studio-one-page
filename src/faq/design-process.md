question: "What's included in your web design process?"
question_he: "מה כלול בתהליך עיצוב האתרים שלכם?"
answer: |
  Our comprehensive design process ensures your website meets your business goals:
  
  **1. Discovery & Planning (Week 1)**
  - In-depth consultation about your business and goals
  - Target audience analysis and competitor research
  - Site architecture and content strategy planning
  - Technical requirements and platform selection
  
  **2. Design & Development (Weeks 2-3)**
  - Custom visual design mockups for approval
  - Mobile-responsive development and coding
  - Content management system integration
  - Performance optimization and testing
  
  **3. Review & Launch (Week 4)**
  - Client review and feedback incorporation
  - Content upload and final quality assurance
  - Domain setup and hosting configuration
  - Launch and post-launch monitoring
  
  **Ongoing Support**
  - 30 days of free updates and bug fixes
  - Content management training session
  - Optional maintenance and hosting packages
  - Priority support for future enhancements
  
  Throughout the process, you'll have regular updates and opportunities to provide feedback to ensure the final result exceeds your expectations.
