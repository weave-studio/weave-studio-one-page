question: "What do I need to prepare before starting my website project?"
question_he: "מה אני צריך להכין לפני שמתחיל את פרויקט האתר שלי?"
answer: |
  Great question! Being prepared helps ensure a smooth project timeline and better results. Here's what to gather before we start:
  
  **Essential Business Information**
  - Business name, address, phone, and email
  - Business hours and service areas
  - Short business description (2-3 sentences)
  - List of services or products you offer
  - Your main business goals for the website
  
  **Content Preparation**
  - Existing website content (if you have one)
  - Brochures, flyers, or marketing materials
  - Product/service descriptions and pricing
  - About us information and company history
  - Staff bios and professional photos (if desired)
  
  **Visual Assets**
  - High-quality business logo (vector format preferred)
  - Professional photos of your products/services
  - Existing brand colors and fonts (if established)
  - Any specific images you want to include
  - Videos or media files (if applicable)
  
  **Technical Requirements**
  - Domain name preference (or existing domain)
  - Email addresses you want to use
  - Social media profiles and accounts
  - Google Analytics or other tracking accounts
  - Any specific features or functionality needs
  
  **Reference and Inspiration**
  - Websites you like and why
  - Competitor websites for reference
  - Specific design styles or layouts you prefer
  - Features from other sites you'd like to include
  
  **Timeline and Budget Information**
  - Preferred launch date or deadline
  - Budget range for the project
  - Ongoing maintenance preferences
  - Future expansion plans
  
  **Don't Worry If You Don't Have Everything**
  - We can help you develop missing content
  - Professional photography can be arranged
  - We'll guide you through domain and hosting setup
  - Content strategy consultation is included
  
  The more prepared you are, the faster we can move through the project, but we're here to help with anything you're missing!
